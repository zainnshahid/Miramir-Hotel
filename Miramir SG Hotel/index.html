FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need to register to book a room?

Yes, you need to register first for your booking it helps us alot.

What amenities are included in your rooms?

Amenities vary depending on the room type you choose. Most rooms include Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a mini-fridge. For specific details, please visit our "Rooms" page or contact us for further information.

Is there parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer street parking for a free fee may also be available nearby.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the rate you book. Please review the details during the booking process.

Do you allow pets?

We understand how much your pet means to you. Unfortunately, our hotel currently does not allow pets on the premises. This is due to a variety of factors, including maintaining a hypoallergenic environment for guests with allergies and ensuring the comfort of all our visitors.
